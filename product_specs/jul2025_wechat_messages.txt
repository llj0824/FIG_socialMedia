一段聊天的录音（比如和朋友之间聊天一下午的录音），把这段录音，我通过“飞书妙记”转成文字稿，在文字稿上，标识了不同的「说话人」、「说话时间」

把这么一篇文字稿，喂给我们的智能体，我们的智能体就能够从里面提炼和定义一些主题，以这些不同的主题，根据聊天的内容和联网知识扩展，生成很多个不同主题的短视频的文字稿，然后拿到智能体生成的这些文字稿，直接录制口播短视频，在表现力不错的情况下，就能做出爆款，这就是一个很好的工具了

A recorded conversation (such as an afternoon chat recording between friends), I use "Feishu Miaoji" to transcribe this audio into text, and on the transcript, it identifies different "speakers" and "speaking times".

Feeding such a transcript to our AI agent, the agent can extract and define themes from it. Based on these different themes, according to the chat content and expanded with internet knowledge, it can generate multiple short video scripts on different topics. Then, taking these AI-generated scripts, you can directly record spoken-word short videos. With decent performance, you can create viral content - this is a great tool.
